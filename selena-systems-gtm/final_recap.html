What Selena Systems sells (the 60-second version)

The Villa Ops OS™ — a $5,000–$9,500 fixed-price, 14-day AI implementation for villa & property management companies: an AI first responder on WhatsApp that answers 95% of guest inquiries within 2 minutes, 24/7, in the guest's language — plus every lead captured in one pipeline, human escalation rules, and a before/after response-time dashboard. Done-for-you on the WhatsApp-and-spreadsheets stack these businesses actually run — the stack every SaaS competitor requires a PMS to serve.

Why this buyer: Bali villa companies are in a documented oversupply squeeze (occupancy ~53%, revenue −16%) where response speed decides who books; they already pay salaries for exactly this work (reservation-agent job posts with 6-hour SLAs are public); and $5–10k ≈ one month of management fees for a mid-size portfolio. Full evidence: market_research.md.

Winner chosen by a 12-offer tournament with advocate/skeptic debates and 3 independent judges (unanimous) — idea_tournament.md. Backups: Bali real-estate concierge (same streets) and Dubai brokerage speed-to-lead (the scale market) — backup_offers.md.

⚠️ Read before doing anything: Task Zero. Selling in person to Bali businesses may not be legal on the founder's current visa — this gates the whole Bali motion and flips Dubai to primary if it fails. One lawyer consult (~$100–500, day 1–2) answers it. The package's default assumption is "not cleared yet": no walk-ins, no local pitching, no publishing the landing page until then. Remote outreach to Dubai and other foreign clients is unaffected — it starts today. Details: winning_offer.md §17 and red_team_report.md (R1, severity 9).
