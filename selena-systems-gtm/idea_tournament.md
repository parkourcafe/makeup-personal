## Step 2/3 — Advocate vs. Skeptic (full briefs in `research/raw/tournament/`)

| Offer | Advocate's strongest point | Skeptic's strongest point |
|---|---|---|
| **O1 Villa Ops** | Only offer sellable in person within 48h; 65 pre-built leads; buyers publish reservation-agent job posts with 6-hour SLAs (the budget line is public); oversupply crunch makes conversion existential; O1 is the prerequisite that unlocks O2 Dubai. | The severity-9 legal gate is the front door, not a footnote — walk-in selling to Indonesian entities is exactly what the visa prohibits; price = 18–28 months of the local salary it replaces; "2 minutes, ever" + 7 deliverables in 14 days solo is overpromise; proof plan is circular. |
| **O2 Dubai** | Double-#1 alignment: top market × top pain; ROI closes on one call (one AED 40k commission repays the fee); cleanest legal path (foreign client, offshore invoice); pipeline of 32k brokers never runs dry. | Self-convicted by its own research: cold remote trust with zero case studies is "the slowest path"; asking a stranger to wire $8–10k offshore fails first contact; agent adoption risk (agents hoard leads on personal WhatsApp — the system attacks their self-interest); realistic close weeks 4–8. |
| **O3 Clinics** | Fastest documented named-business pain (Rejuvie "radio silence", 42% booking cliff); spans two top-5 markets; compliance guardrails as differentiator. | **Fatal (all 3 judges):** Bali clinics are locally licensed Indonesian medical entities — no offshore-billing dodge exists, killing the fast half; health-communication liability + AHPRA makes zero-case-study status disqualifying; "patient in pain" is triage, not logistics. |
| **O4 Bali RE Concierge** | Same in-person motion as O1 with richer buyer math (~$15k/deal commission, USD-linked); demo built on the agency's own listings before meeting two; a win is the exact demo asset Dubai requires. | Legal gate applies equally; ~5% commission is an unverified assumption; 6-month buyer cycles delay the provable case study everything depends on; a bot one hallucination from misstating leasehold law to a $299k buyer. |
| **O5 Agency Autopilot** | Budget is public (coordinator hiring posts); agencies buy productized services natively; only compounding/repeat-purchase offer; cleanest payments. | Overrides the research's own synthesis (US/EU = "later, referral-driven market"); wedge mismatch — agencies are API-native and professionally served by SaaS; buyers are professional vendor-evaluators who will ask the unanswerable case-study question; integration sprawl breaks fixed scope. |

## Step 4 — Judge verdicts (full verdicts in `research/raw/tournament/judge-{1,2,3}.md`)

| Judge (lens) | O1 | O2 | O3 | O4 | O5 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| J1 — Speed to first check | **82** | 61 | 49 | 68 | 36 |
| J2 — Risk-adjusted 6-month EV | **78** | 70 | 34 | 56 | 44 |
| J3 — Buyer psychology / sales reality | **74** | 49 | 28 | 66 | 37 |
| **Aggregate** | **234** | 180 | 111 | 190 | 117 |

**Unanimous #1: O1 Villa Ops OS.** All three judges independently converged on the same play:

1. **O1 as the Days 1–14 primary**, with three judge-mandated modifications baked into the winning offer:
   - **Task Zero — entity screening:** qualify the 65-lead list for offshore/foreign-billing entities *before* selling (J2: screenable in ~2 days for ~$500 of legal advice; go/no-go threshold ≥8–10 billable leads, Dubai as fallback).
   - **v1 scope cut:** responder + escalation + pipeline as the core promise; owner reporting and upsell flows as fast-follow, so 14-day solo delivery is honest (answers the O1 skeptic's overpromise attack).
   - **Trust-laddered payment:** live demo on the prospect's own villa → paid pilot/milestone structure at founding-client pricing with a case-study clause, replacing an unwinnable 100%-upfront ask (J3).
2. **O4 as backup 1** — same walk-in circuit, ~80% shared build; run as the side-door on the same route.
3. **O2 as backup 2 and the scale market** — run async from day 1 (demo video + Trustpilot complaint mining), expect close in weeks 4–8 once the Bali before/after numbers exist.
4. **O3 dropped entirely** (the only offer where a skeptic argument was judged fatal by all three judges). **O5 shelved to Days 60+** as the repeat-purchase channel once case studies exist.
