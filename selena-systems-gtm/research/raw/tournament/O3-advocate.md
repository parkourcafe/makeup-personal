The urgency is structural, not seasonal. No-shows alone cost physio clinics up to A$114,827 per clinic per year (peer-reviewed study, §3) — that's a daily bleed, not a someday problem. And in Bali, oversupply-driven professionalization (§2) means clinics competing for international patients cannot afford to be the one that goes silent — the patient literally messages the clinic next door.
